Temple Run

The player controls a running character in this never-ending game, and they have to dodge attackers and obstacles. The main story of the game is that the player controls a figure who takes a golden idol from what looks to be an Aztec temple located in a swamp. In Temple Run, your primary goal is to escape the pursuing apes while gathering as many gold, red, and blue coins as you can to obtain the maximum score.

5 reasons why this game is my favorite:

  • The game helps me kill off the time.
  • It increases my speed in thinking.
  • It is simple to play but hard to master.
  • The game controls are really easy to execute.
  • The level increases over time which makes it more exciting.
